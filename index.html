01 Data We Collect

TabLX stores the following data exclusively in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local), which is sandboxed to the extension and never leaves your device unless explicitly described below:

DataWhere it's storedPurpose
Tab sessionsLocal device onlySaving and restoring your workspaces
Session notes & tagsLocal device onlyLabels and annotations you add
User preferencesLocal device onlyDisplay and behaviour settings
License keyLocal device onlyActivating Pro features
License status cacheLocal device onlyAvoiding repeated API calls (24h TTL)

The extension makes limited outbound requests to two external services:

ServiceData SentPurpose
api.whop.comYour license keyPro license validation
google.com/s2/faviconsTab domain onlyDisplaying tab favicons in the UI

02 What We Don't Collect

TabLX has no backend server of its own. No data is ever transmitted to us.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not track your browsing history beyond what you explicitly save as a session
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting tools
  • We do not use cookies
  • We do not share, sell, or transfer data to any third parties beyond the two services listed in Section 1

03 How Data Is Used

Every piece of data TabLX stores has a single, specific purpose and is never used for anything else:

DataUse
Saved sessionsRestoring your tab workspaces on demand
License keyVerifying active Pro membership with Whop — once per 24 hours
License cachePreventing redundant API calls and ensuring offline access
Tab domain (favicon)Displaying the correct icon for each saved tab in the UI

04 Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device and persists until one of the following occurs:

  • You manually delete a session within the extension
  • You click "Remove License" in the extension settings
  • You uninstall the extension — the browser automatically clears all local storage

We have no access to your locally stored data and cannot retrieve, modify, or delete it on your behalf.

05 Third-Party Services

The two external services TabLX contacts are governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Whop — whop.com/privacy — governs how Whop processes your license key during validation
  • Google — policies.google.com/privacy — governs Google's favicon service

06 Children's Privacy

TabLX is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the extension, please contact us so we can address it.
